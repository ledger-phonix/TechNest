Community Guidelines

Help us maintain a respectful, secure, and productive environment for every TechNest member.

Purpose of the Community

TechNest is built to support collaboration between IT students, professionals, and technology companies in Multan. Our goal is to encourage meaningful connections, learning, and career development within a trusted and focused tech community.

Registration Process

To access TechNest features, users must select a specific track during registration:

  • Individual: For students and tech professionals.
  • Company: For local startups and established firms.
  • Each account is linked to a single role to ensure system integrity.
  • Profile completion is required to appear in matching results.

Profile Accuracy

Trust is our core value. Members are expected to provide accurate information. Misrepresentation of skills, identity, or company details may result in restricted access or account removal.

Communication Guidelines

Our temporary real-time chat is designed for professional networking. Please follow these rules:

  • Maintain a professional and respectful tone at all times.
  • No harassment, hate speech, or abusive language.
  • No spam or irrelevant commercial promotions.
  • Shared files must be safe, relevant, and professional.

Acceptable Use

Misuse of the platform—including unauthorized access attempts, data scraping, or system abuse—is strictly prohibited and will be reported.

Company Responsibilities

Companies should post genuine opportunities and interact transparently. Job and internship postings must be clear, accurate, and relevant to the local tech community.

Enforcement

TechNest reserves the right to restrict or terminate access for users who violate these guidelines to maintain the integrity and safety of our community.
