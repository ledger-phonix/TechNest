Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the TechNest community and how we connect talent with opportunity.

General

TechNest is a community-based platform specifically designed to bridge the gap between IT students, tech professionals, and local companies in Multan. We help students find internships and companies find the right talent through a match-driven system.

No, TechNest is free for students and individuals looking to grow their careers and connect with the local tech ecosystem.

Matching & Opportunities

Our system uses a predefined catalog of professions and skills. When your profile skills match the requirements of a company's job or internship posting, you'll appear in their discovery feed, making it easier for them to reach out to you.

Yes. Registered companies have a dedicated dashboard where they can post internships and job openings, track applications, and discover local talent based on specific technical needs.

Communication & Security

To ensure privacy and meaningful connections, chat rooms are created only when both the individual and the company are online and a match has been established. For security, these temporary chat rooms are deleted once the session is closed.

Absolutely. We use secure authentication and role-based access control. Your data is stored in a secure MySQL database, and we only share relevant profile details with matched companies.
