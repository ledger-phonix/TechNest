Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how TechNest collects, uses, and protects user information within our community.

Introduction

TechNest is a community-based technology platform designed to connect individuals and companies in Multan. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle personal data provided by users while using our services to ensure a secure professional environment.

Information We Collect

To provide a match-driven experience, we may collect the following:

  • Account information such as email address and role
  • Profile details including skills, profession, and location
  • Company-related information provided during registration
  • Temporary metadata for real-time communication

How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage user and company accounts
  • To enable skill-based matching between members and companies
  • To facilitate real-time communication between online users
  • To improve platform functionality and user experience

Chat and Communication

TechNest provides temporary real-time chat functionality. In accordance with our security protocols, chat messages and shared files are not permanently stored on our servers and are deleted once the chat session ends or the users go offline.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures. Passwords are securely hashed using modern algorithms and never stored in plain text. Access to sensitive data is strictly restricted based on role-based access control (RBAC).

Third-Party Services

TechNest does not sell, trade, or share personal information with third-party services. Any external links provided within the community are subject to the privacy policies of their respective providers.

User Responsibility

Users are responsible for providing accurate information and for maintaining the confidentiality of their login credentials. We recommend using strong, unique passwords for your TechNest account.

Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated as the TechNest platform evolves. We encourage users to check this page periodically for any changes.

Contact

If you have questions regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please reach out to the team through our contact page.
